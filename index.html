About The Project

EcoWave is a comprehensive mobile and web application designed to empower individuals to report marine pollution, join community cleanup events, and learn about ocean sustainability. By integrating reporting tools, educational resources, and gamified community action into a single ecosystem, EcoWave aims to transform awareness into measurable environmental impact.

Key Functionalities

Bridging technology and marine conservation.

Pollution Reporting

Real-time reporting using photos, descriptions, and GPS data to identify pollution hotspots and alert authorities.

Cleanup Events

Discover, organize, and join local cleanup missions. Connect with NGOs and volunteers to take collective action.

EcoPulse AI

AI-powered assistant powered by Gemini to identify pollutants, provide safety tips, and answer conservation queries.

Education Hub

Access a library of articles, videos, and infographics to learn about marine life and sustainable practices.

Gamification

Earn points, unlock badges, and climb the leaderboard by actively participating in reporting and events.

User Profile

Track your impact streak, view your contribution history, and manage your environmental guardian journey.
